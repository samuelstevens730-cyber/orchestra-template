# Open Loops

> **Purpose:** Unresolved items, pending questions, and things that need follow-up.
> **Update frequency:** At session close. Add new items as they surface; mark resolved items as CLOSED.
> **Owner:** Gemini maintains. Anyone can add items.
> **Rule:** Don't delete closed items immediately — move them to the bottom under "Recently Closed" for one cycle, then prune.
